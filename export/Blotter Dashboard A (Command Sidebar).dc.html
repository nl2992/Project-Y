Purpose
Auto-detect whether the uploaded file is a legacy multi-sheet workbook or a cleaned flat workbook, then normalise all rows into one reporting model with headline, raw rebuild and variance views.
Inputs
· Legacy workbook with curated summary tabs and blotter tabs
· Cleaned flat workbook with tier fields, trade date, volume, PC, VA/GNBV and comment metadata
· Live USDHKD from the FX spot time-series endpoint, fallback 7.8430
Outputs
· Headline, raw rebuild and variance summaries
· Structured FI, Illiquid / Repack, Collar and Equity TRS detail tabs
· CSV export and print-ready summary
Total policy
· FX is included in totals by default
· Switchable to ex-FX at any time
· All three summary modes follow the selected policy
TRS headline FX override
· Default source is the live USDHKD feed
· Manual override available for controlled re-runs
· TRS headline notional converts HKD local notional at the selected rate
Mode definitions
· Headline — curated workbook headline where available
· Raw Rebuild — rebuilt directly from unified rows
· Variance — raw rebuild minus headline
